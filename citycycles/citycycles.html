CITY CYCLES | WEBSITE REDESIGN CONCEPT

OBJECTIVE

Improve the user experience of City Cycles existing website to increase the booking of online bike reservations.

The Problem

City Cycles bicycle rental customers do not like to use the online reservation feature on the City Cycles website. Instead, people still prefer to call or stop by in-person to make a reservation. Unfortunately, this has created a high number of phone calls and drop-ins, making it difficult for City Cycles to provide quality customer service. City Cycles was unsure why people weren’t booking online and hired me as a UX Designer to further investigate. I was hired to improve City Cycles’ user experience, making the online biking reservation process easier and more convenient for City Cycles, thus increasing the number of online reservations.

The Solution

  • Create a CTA (call-to-action) button to begin filling the reservation form online as soon as you are on the homepage
  • Create a rental page tab in the nav bar to be able to easily access rentals from anywhere on the site
  • Redesign the website to lead users through a short 3 click reservation process with an instant confirmation page & mobile app details
  • Results:
  • made the site more intuitive for users to easily book online reservations
  • eliminated confusion, frustration & annoyance of users needing to call or make reservations in person
  • 3-step reservation process increased the online reservation rates by 50%
  • City Cycles staff is now able to increase exceptional levels of customer service in new areas of business since now relieved of high volume reservation calls & in-store reservations
